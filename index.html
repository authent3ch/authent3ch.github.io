mission

I've seen the future, and I'm here to help you thrive in it.

I'm on a mission to "Make Main Street Great Again" by empowering competitive emerging businesses with Wall Street automation and Silicon Valley analytics to preserve and strengthen free-market driven innovation.

As an enterprise workflow automation and data engineer with nearly a decade of intimate experience at organizations including Goldman Sachs, UBS, Facebook, and Netflix I've seen what I needed to see.

Data is the oil of the 21st century, and privacy is the world's fastest diminishing yet exponentially valuable commodity.

The scale and velocity of automation implentation, A.I integration, and big data harvesting happening on the public domain at the enterprise level is unbelievable, ultimately rendering control and ownership of the new digital frontier in the hands of very small few. Sound familiar? Think natural resources + oil & gas at the turn of the 20th century.

My goal for content creation is to de-mystify tech, connect with like-minded creators, catalyze technical creativity, and diversify the demographic of tech talent around the globe.

A.I. and automation alone have the potential to usher in the New Age - eliminating the 40 hour work week, curb the housing crisis, and curtail the globe's current retrograde trajectory.
